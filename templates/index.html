/api/v1.0/precipitation -Date & precipitation

/api/v1.0/stations -Return list of station names

/api/v1.0/tobs -Return temperatures for last 12 months of Entries

/api/v1.0/start-date/end-date -Return temperatures for desired dates

Enter /api/v1.0/start-date/9999 to find avg, min, max temps since start date

Ex: /api/v1.0/2012-02-02/9999

Enter /api/v1.0/start/end to find avg, min, max temps between dates

Ex: /api/v1.0/2012-02-02/2014-02-02
